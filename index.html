Skillshare Gamification App

Attractive and intuitive data visualization app for users to monitor their learning.

Be The Main Character In Your Learning Process With Skillshare

Features

Summary Bar

When users finish watching a lesson or a class, they will get points that will allow them to increase their score and level up. In this section, users will see their score, the number of classes and lessons they have completed, the number of days in a row they have used the platform and the skill with the highest growth in the current week.

Road Map

There are six levels associated to ranges of score. Users will be able to visualize their progress in this road as they spend more time in the platform, getting a mental and positional representation of their progress.

Visualizations

Bar chart:
Allows users to see the scores of the last 7 days they have visited the platform. They could follow their daily progress, having points of comparison to get pills of motivation to keep pushing.
Radar chart:
Displays the top six skills with the highest scores, giving the Skillshare user’s avatar a video-game style characterization. Showing continuous changes related to their avatar’s skills’ development, fostering appropriation of the feeling of progress and learning.
Bubble chart:
Displays the 30 skills with the highest scores, providing a global perspective of the skills acquired and the amount of time spent on them. This gives users a sense of accomplishment.

About

The use of online learning platforms has skyrocketed in the last years. One of the reasons these platforms attract users is their flexibility. This flexibility comes with a challenge: keeping users engaged and motivated to finish their courses since there is no pressure to do so. This is why we looked for inspiration in the dashboards of video games and we studied theories of motivation to design a profile page where users can visualize the time they have invested in learning and the skills they have acquired. These visuals of how much they have worked gives users a sense of accomplishment that boosts their motivation.
